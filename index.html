About Me

My undergraduate studies in Computer Science and Engineering have given me a solid grounding in programming, algorithms, and problem-solving. These are skills I'm really excited to apply to the world of robotics. What truly fascinates me is how we can make robots smarter by integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI). I'm particularly drawn to exploring how AI can help robots understand their surroundings, make decisions on their own, and even work alongside people. I'm eager to learn more about things like how robots can "see" and "learn" using AI, and how we can design AI systems that work well in the real world. The idea of using robotics to solve challenges and make our lives better is incredibly motivating for me, and I'm keen to keep learning and exploring in this dynamic field.
